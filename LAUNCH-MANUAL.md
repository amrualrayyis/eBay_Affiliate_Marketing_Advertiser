# 🚀 littlniss Empire: Launch Manual

Welcome to your automated AI empire. This system is designed to run in your sleep, finding high-value deals, branding them, and preparing them for social media.

## 📁 File Reference
*   **`auto-deals-bot.js`**: The main engine. Finds deals, generates AI content, brands images, and posts to Discord.
*   **`research-niches.js`**: The brain. Searches for trending topics and updates your categories.
*   **`price-watchdog.js`**: The monitor. Alerts you if a previous deal goes on sale.
*   **`ceo-report.js`**: The analyst. Sends you a weekly performance and profit summary.
*   **`build-hub.js`**: The webmaster. Generates your visual storefront (`index.html`).
*   **`build-newsletter.js`**: The publisher. Creates your weekly style digest.

## 📅 Daily & Weekly Routine

### Daily (Automated)
*   Run **`RUN-DAILY-BOT.bat`**: Finds 10 fresh deals, brands them, and prepares your pins.
*   Run **`RUN-PRICE-WATCHDOG.bat`**: Checks if your previously posted deals have dropped in price.

### Weekly Maintenance
*   Run **`RUN-WEEKLY-MAINTENANCE.bat`**: This single command handles everything to keep your empire fresh:
    1.  Refreshes your niches with new AI trends.
    2.  Performs competitive intelligence.
    3.  Generates your CEO performance report.
    4.  Builds your weekly style newsletter.

## 📈 Week 1 Strategy: "The Traffic Loop"
1.  **Bio Link**: Put your Pinterest profile URL (`pinterest.com/litlniss1`) in your Instagram and TikTok bio.
2.  **Pinterest SEO**: Ensure you have boards named exactly like your niches (e.g., "littlniss Home Decor").
3.  **Consistency**: Even if you don't feel like making a video, let the bot keep pinning to Pinterest. The algorithm rewards consistency over everything else.
4.  **Verification**: Check your **eBay Partner Network** dashboard every Friday to see which niche is getting the most clicks.

## 🛠️ Maintenance
*   **Memory**: The system automatically clears old item IDs after 500 posts to stay fast.
*   **Logs**: Your history is safely stored in `littlniss-deals-log.csv`. Keep this file; it's the DNA of your business.
*   **Images**: Downloaded assets are in the `/posts` folder. You can manually delete these once a month if your hard drive gets full.
